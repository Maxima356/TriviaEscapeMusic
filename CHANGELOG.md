## [1.0.0] - 2025-02-27

### Added
- Initial release of TriviaEscapeMusic
- Custom apparatus escape sequence:
  - Map-wide alarm when apparatus is taken
  - Trivia OST (reversed "Sunshine Day" from Tower Unite)
  - 3 random alarm sound variations
- Progressive intensity mechanics:
  - Music speeds up after 1 minute
  - Alarm gets progressively louder
- Nuclear ending at 1:50 with explosion
- Integration with PizzaTowerEscapeMusic base mod
- Created for LethalReloaded modpack (works standalone)
